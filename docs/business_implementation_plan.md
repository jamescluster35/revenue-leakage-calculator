# BDL CRM & Leads Monetization Plan

This document outlines the step-by-step strategy to generate revenue using BDL Leads Pro and the CRM Revenue Leakage Calculator, without requiring any custom software development.

---

## Phase 1: The "Booking Setup Consultant" Model

When a restaurant owner responds to your email audit and wants to fix their no-show and vacancy problems:

### The Offer
You act as an implementation consultant. Instead of writing code, you set up and optimize existing industry-standard software.

### Implementation Steps
1. **Identify the System**: Recommend **Resy**, **Tock**, or **OpenTable** (which allow credit card holds or prepaid deposits to eliminate no-shows).
2. **Determine the Fee**: Charge a flat **Setup & Optimization Fee** ($500 - $1,500).
3. **Scope of Work**:
   * Create and configure their reservation account.
   * Upload their floor plan and table layout.
   * Write and implement their cancellation policy (e.g., "$10 fee for cancellations within 24 hours").
   * Install the reservation widget on their website and link it to their Google Business Profile and social media.

---

## Phase 2: Operational Consulting & Revenue Audits

Use the CRM Calculator as a sales hook to offer non-technical operational audits.

### The Offer
Provide a custom "Revenue Recovery Plan" showing the owner how to stop losing money on empty tables and food waste.

### Implementation Steps
1. **Interactive Audit**: Schedule a 30-minute Zoom call to run their numbers through the CRM calculator together.
2. **Deliverable**: Provide a personalized PDF report (generated by your Google Apps Script calculator tool) with actionable advice:
   * **No-Show Prevention**: Simple text-reminder sequence rules.
   * **Food Waste Auditing**: Recommending simple tracking templates or portion-control guides.
3. **Fee**: Charge a flat audit fee ($250 - $500) or package it as a monthly advisory service.

---

## Phase 3: B2B Lead Generation (Leads-as-a-Service)

*This is the fastest path to scaling because you already have the python scrapers and the Outlook outreach scripts working on your computer.*

### The Target Senders
Target other businesses that sell to restaurants, such as:
* Local restaurant suppliers and food distributors.
* POS (Point of Sale) sales representatives.
* Commercial kitchen hood cleaners and grease-trap service providers.
* Social media marketing agencies specializing in hospitality.

### The Offer
*"I will scrape, verify, and run cold outreach campaigns to local restaurants to get you booked sales meetings with owners."*

### Implementation Steps
1. **Package**:
   * **Base Fee**: $500/month (covers email account setup and script maintenance).
   * **Performance Fee**: $100 per booked meeting.
2. **Execution**: Use your existing `compile_restaurants.py` and `run_outlook_campaign.ps1` scripts, modifying only the email body to pitch the client's B2B services instead of your calculator.
